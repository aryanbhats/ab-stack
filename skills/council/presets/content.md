## Consensus Rules
Priority order when personas conflict: accuracy > brand voice > audience resonance > conversion > growth. A persona higher in the priority chain can override a lower one. If Brand Guardian flags a voice violation, it outweighs a Conversion Expert's suggestion to change tone. The user acts as final judge.
